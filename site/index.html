How it works

  1. TradingView — Add the QuantLens indicator to your chart.
  2. Create an alert — Condition: the study, Event: Any alert(), Timing: once per bar close.
  3. Deliver — Webhook to your collector or copy from Alert Log.
  4. Store — Save as /symbol/tf/epoch.json.
  5. Analyze — Feed the JSON + prompt to your LLM. Instant TA.

Tip: Scale across a watchlist with multi‑chart layouts and a single webhook endpoint that accepts all symbols.

What the LLM sees at a glance

Market state

Session, anomaly flags, health, ATR rank. Tight vs explosive tape in a sentence.

Trend & momentum

SMA/EMA alignment, RSI, ADX/DI, OBV slope. Clear bias per horizon.

Volume structure

POC magnet, value acceptance, discovery vs balance. Where moves are likely to stick.

Actionable scenarios

Breakout, rejection, or range. Triggers, invalidations, stops, and TPs — pre‑wired.
